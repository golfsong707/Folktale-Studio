 Ìgálâ Folktale Pedagogical Studio
**Reimagining Cultural Heritage through AI-Powered Pedagogy**

## Project Overview
The Ìgálâ Folktale Pedagogical Studio is an AI-driven educational ecosystem that preserves and disseminates traditional Ìgálâ oral narratives. By bridging the gap between ancestral wisdom and modern technology, we transform the oral folktale *Tortoise in the Land of Women* (TILOW) into a high-fidelity, interactive, and cross-curricular learning resource.

## The Problem
Oral traditions are at risk of being lost in a digital-first world. Educators in low-resource environments lack the tools to bridge the gap between cultural storytelling and modern academic curriculum (Science, Civics, Linguistics), and they lack access to high-quality, culturally-authenticated creative assets.

## The Solution: AI as a Creative Partner
Our platform uses AI not as a replacement, but as a **Creative Partner** that:
*   **Generates Culturally Authentic Imagery:** Using IBM Granite to produce visuals that honor the Ìgálâ aesthetic.
*   **Automates Linguistic Annotation:** Simplifying the complex ELAN-style morphosyntactic glossing.
*   **Democratizes Pedagogy:** Converting digital narratives into offline-ready, printable educational materials for schools without internet access.

## IBM Technology Stack
- **Development Tool:** [IBM Bob](http://ibm.biz/university-bob) (Primary orchestrator and development partner).
- **Inference Engine:** [IBM Granite](https://github.com/ibm-granite-community) for structured reasoning and image prompt engineering.
- **Workflow:** [LangChain & LangFlow](https://www.langflow.org) for managing complex pedagogical agentic chains.
- **Infrastructure:** Built with Next.js, React, and Python for a premium, dark-mode-first user experience.
